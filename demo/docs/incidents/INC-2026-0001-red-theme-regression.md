## Incident INC-2026-0001

Title: Temporary red redesign caused confusion and failed review

Date: 2026-02-18
Severity: medium
Service: marketing-site

### Summary

A redesign branch changed the primary buttons, hero accents, and badge styling from green to red.

### What went wrong

Reviewers interpreted the red primary buttons as destructive actions instead of a positive call to action.
The brand team also rejected the build because it broke the agreed green-first identity.

### Resolution

The release was rolled back.
Primary buttons, hero gradients, and badges were restored to green.

### Permanent rule

Keep the primary brand color green.
Do not change the global styling from green to red.
